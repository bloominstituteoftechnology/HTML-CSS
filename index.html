About Me

My name is Adam Fetters and i'm obsessed with technology. Ever since I was 3 and my parents bought me a Tandy 1000, I would play all the time. (You remember the one with the cartridges, attached keyboard, and a little joystick that had 2 buttons, if you were lucky). I attended DeVry University on a partial academic scholorship in 1999 with a major in Electronics Engineering Technology. After a year I had a 4.0, but I dropped out after getting a great job, as I wasn't mature enough to realize the value of education. I ended up living in Columbus, Ohio for 5 years, where I got experience in marketing, sales, I.T., and even worked at a gym for a little while. Great for me, as I loved to workout. I found my way back into college at a later time in my life where I graduated with a degree in Audio Engineering / Video Production. I shot some commericals, worked on a few short films, low budget projects, but never really got going in that industry. I always dabbled in coding, html, css, but never really knew which direction to go or was fully invested. After years of doing manual labor including masonry, factory, painting, along with playing poker on the side, I decided to get back into programming and joined a bootcamp in Memphis, Tennessee. It was fast paced, and I struggled with the JavaScript aspect, and ended up moving back home. I continued building on that education, and after a few months started to get the hang of javascript. After googling "Get paid to code", I stumbled upon Lambda School, where I know am in my 3rd week and loving it! I have learned more in this time, than my whole life, and have finally figured out what makes me wake up every morning. Coding!
